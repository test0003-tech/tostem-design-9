About Tostem

WHAT MAKES TOSTEM A LEADING BRAND

TOSTEM is developed by LIXIL, one of the world's largest housing and building materials companies. Leveraging decades of Japanese innovation, TOSTEM delivers pre-engineered system windows and doors that combine superior aesthetics with unmatched performance.

Every TOSTEM product undergoes rigorous testing and quality assurance, ensuring they meet the highest global standards for thermal insulation, sound reduction, water resistance, and wind load capacity.

With TEXGUARD surface protection technology, TOSTEM products maintain their beauty for years, resisting UV degradation, scratching, and environmental wear. Our products are designed to withstand India's diverse climate conditions while maintaining their elegance and functionality.

TOSTEM's pre-engineered system ensures consistent quality across every installation. Each component is precisely manufactured and tested as a complete system, unlike conventional site-assembled windows that can vary in quality and performance.

From concept to completion, TOSTEM provides end-to-end solutions including design consultation, manufacturing, delivery, and professional installation through our certified dealer network across India.
